5 common mistakes to avoid when hiring a Virtual Assistant

April 19

Hiring a virtual assistant the right way is critical to ensuring that you reap all the benefits that come with them. Otherwise, they might become another problem you may have to solve. These are some general tips to help you avoid mistakes when hiring a virtual assistant. Here are a few of them.


1. Failing to understand the usefulness of a virtual assistant


Virtual assistants are useful for a purpose but if you don’t know their purpose, it will be difficult to hire the right one to start with. A bad way to go about hiring a virtual assistant is anticipating business growth without having any clear task for them to do. A bad move would be to hire virtual assistants because other businesses are doing so. In both instances, you would not know which specific personality or skill sets to look out for when hiring.


2. Failing to provide the proper training


When hiring a virtual assistant, you must take into consideration the need to train them. Virtual assistants do not know your business as you do. It is your duty to bring them up to speed. It is also your responsibility to give them clear instructions on the kinds of tasks you want them to undertake. This would help you glean the best from them, saving you time and money. This means you must be patient, provide constant feedback, avoid vagueness and communicate constantly with your assistant.


3. Not providing a qualifying framework


You should never hire a virtual assistant simply because they have an impressive resume. Just as it is with hiring any employee, a workable system must govern the screening of virtual assistants. Consider an interview, an evaluation of personal qualities and skill sets and a behavioural test to assess them holistically. This would give you a better picture of the candidate you seek to hire. Do your due diligence at this stage to prevent having to do the job all over again. You would have to do all of these if you are hiring a virtual assistant yourself but if you request the services of a virtual assistant from a virtual assistant company, you would not have to go through any of this process. They would take care of it for you.


4. Not considering enough candidates


Don’t hire the very first candidate you interview just because they impressed you. Trust me, several others will amaze you. Wait until you have a pool of candidates and carefully compare all their personal qualities, skill sets and experience to get yourself the ideal fit for your business. A virtual assistant company like Colibri also allows you to change a virtual assistant if you are not satisfied with your first choice.


5. Lack of a clear communication


A virtual assistant would not be with you all the time. This does not mean that you should not set up some good quality control measures and constantly take progress reports. There are communication channels you can use for voice calls, video calls and emails; you can establish constant communication with the virtual assistant. This is critical for a good working relationship and an appreciation of your business needs.


Conclusion


If you must free up your time and improve productivity with virtual assistants, avoiding common mistakes that most people make would save you heartaches. It is possible to have a great working relationship with virtual assistants who will in turn work with you for your personal and business success when you hire correctly.
