Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who is a virtual assistant?

A virtual assistant (who could also be a personal assistant or an executive assistant) is someone who helps you accomplish your tasks from anywhere they are. They provide you the needed support to help you boost your efficiency and increase your productivity when you hire them or outsource your work to them.

2. How long does it take to get a virtual assistant?

Depending on your availability, you can get a verified and qualified virtual assistant working with you within 2 - 3 days.

3.How much does it cost to get a virtual assistant?

The cost of getting a virtual assistant is based on your specific needs. We promise that you won’t have to break the bank to get a great one. We would also consider how long you would like to work with the virtual assistant.

4. Can I renew my subscription for a virtual assistant?

Yes, you can renew your subscription for a virtual assistant. You can also opt in for monthly subscriptions or stretch your subscription to cover months. You get discounts for long subscriptions.

5. What if I need a virtual assistant for less than a month?

You can work with a virtual for as short as one week.It doesn’t have to be a month.

6. Do I have to sign a contract?

Our contracts protect you and your business as it protects the virtual assistant. We want both parties to always be safe work

7.Can I have more than one virtual assistant?

Yes. Depending on your preference and the volume of tasks you cannot handle all by yourself, you can get more than one virtual assistant to help you out.

8. I don’t need a virtual assistant for myself, I only want to outsource my tasks to a virtual assistant, how do I go about it?

We believe that you need this for short-term tasks that might probably not take up to a week. In that case, you can outsource them to a virtual assistant and we would manage the process of ensuring that you get quality work within a short turnaround time (we beat deadlines and exceed your expectations).
