Our Impact

Africa has a young, vibrant, and growing population of 420 million young people, which is expected to double over the next 30 years. Unfortunately, 71% of these young people live on less than $2 a day and 60% of them are either unemployed or underemployed. Faced with this problem, we have developed a solution to allow the greatest number of young talents, who have little or no means and opportunities, to access a job according to their level of studies, their professional skills or simply their know-how and their training. Thanks to digital technology.

Give work, give the future

At Colibri Africa, we believe that everyone has usable talents and skills. Thus, we encourage our workers to offer their services according to their preferences and their know-how. The goal of having a positive impact on the lives of assistants and their families drives every facet of our business. Because of this focus, we see our Colibri assistants working hard to make a tangible impact on our customers through incredible work, and it's a whole virtuous cycle that makes our business grow.

Improve skills for career development

With a skills upgrade focused on technical skills, and soft skills, we help our team members launch long-term careers.

Earn a better life

We believe that connecting people to decent digital work and paying them what they are worth can help them improve their lives and well-being for themselves and their families and solve some of the world's most pressing challenges.
